ABA Youth Camp 2016


Our 4th annual Youth Camp is scheduled for the weekend of 18th to 20th March, and we are hoping to build on the tradition we have established in order to reach new heights, including selecting a prestigious Auckland Youth Representative Band from camp applicants. We have an amazing line-up of tutors and two fantastic conductors in Billy Middleton and Robert Simpson. The camp is for all ability levels as it is our quest to fill 2 bands, one advanced and one beginner level to cater for all.

To register your expressions of interest, click here.

Please take a look at our attached posters for all the details of this camp including our original promo, draft itinerary, and 'Meet the Tutors' page. If you have any queries, please do not hesitate to send these through the expressions of interest form, and we will respond to you with specific feedback to help you make your mind up.
