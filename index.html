Welcome to the Auckland Bands Association Website

The Auckland Bands Association (ABA) helps member brass bands with education, band competitions, solo competitions and encourages the formation of new bands in the Auckland and Northland areas of New Zealand. We are linked to BBANZ the Brass Bands Association of New Zealand - the national body for New Zealand brass bands.

From this website you can see news and events throughout the area, contact a local band, see contest results, apply to enter a contest, apply to be part of our highly successful youth programmes and seek helpful information from our team.
